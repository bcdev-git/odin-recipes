Cow Offal

Moooo! Nothing screams flavor like cow innards! From mouth-watering stomach meat to slippery intestines, there's a part of the cow for everyone. Now you can bring this delicacy to your dinner table!

Ingredients

  • Cow (for the internal organs)
  • Your favorite seasoning or soy sauce
  • A hearty appetite!

Steps

  1. Find a cow
  2. Assert dominance by mooing at it loudly
  3. Butcher the cow and remove all its organs
  4. Prepare your favorite sauce or other seasonings
  5. Chow down!

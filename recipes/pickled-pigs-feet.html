Pickled Pigs Feet

Pickled pigs feet are a delicacy with nearly universal acclaim. With a pig and a little know-how, you too can create your own succulent pigs feet!

Ingredients

  • Pig (for the feet)
  • Pickling solution
  • A hearty appetite!

Steps

  1. Find a pig
  2. Flash the pig a cheeky grin
  3. Remove its feet via your favorite method
  4. Pickle said feet
  5. Consume the feet, preferably with a nice bottle of red

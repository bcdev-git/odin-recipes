Basashi (Raw Horse Meat)

Nei~gh! Horse, it's what's for dinner! You'll be galloping straight to culinary bliss with this delectable recipe for raw horse meat!

Ingredients

  • A horse
  • Your favorite dipping sauce. Soy sauce recommended.
  • A hearty appetite!

Steps

  1. Find a horse
  2. Give it a pat and a knowing smile
  3. Butcher it via your favorite method
  4. Cut the raw meat into small strips
  5. Prepare some soy sauce or your favorite seasoning
  6. Enjoy this succulent delight!
